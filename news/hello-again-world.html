1st February 2022

Hello Again, World

Clang Sayne has been having a Big Sleep.
The pandemic and pregnancy aligned perfectly here in Wexford: my daughter is now 14 months old, and the world seems to be returning from the brink of pandemicdom. Time to start getting my Clang Sayne ducks in line again.
What better day to do so than Feb 1st: officially the first day of Spring in Ireland. A tide of 'by the wind sailor' jellies washed in on the beach this morning, plus a lot of very pink carageen moss – nice to see some colours again after the long winter. Here's to imminent new beginnings!
