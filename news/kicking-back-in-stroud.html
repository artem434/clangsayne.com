Monday 9th July

The Prince Albert Inn, Stroud: Weds 25th July

We're going to play at the very beautiful
Prince Albert Inn . in the Cotswolds. Upon arrival, we will collectively assume the name "Underhill".
I anticipate our mysteriously disappearing half-way through a song, and our rooms being raided by ringwraiths in the night. If someone could please summon Led Zepplin to play 'No Quarter' as we make our way to the venue under cloak of darkness that would be greatly enhance the whole experience.
