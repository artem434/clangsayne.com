Opening Ergodos' Santa Rita Series

Thrilled to be opening the fourth season in the Ergodos label's Santa Rita Series. Taking place at the Little Museum on Stephen's Green, Dublin, this night has been running now since 2015 and has featured a steady rollcall of cutting edge artists. Each evening kicks off at 7pm with a Santa Rita wine tasting, and a fireside interview with the artists. The performances take place at 8pm in the museum’s beautifully resonant downstairs gallery. The space is candlelit and the atmosphere highly intimate – a concentrated ritual shared by performer and audience.
