Sounding Seams documentary release, feat. Clang Sayne

I'll soon be releasing this short documentary I made with Crannog Media following my Sounding Seams project from its inception in 2018 to the closing residency with Clang Sayne at the Irish National Heritage Park in June 2022.

A big shout-out to all the wonderful people who took part in Sounding Seams and helped make it happen - too many to list here; you know who you are!

I’ll be releasing the EP of four songs we recorded in the crannog over the coming weeks. Watch this space!
