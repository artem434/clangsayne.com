2nd May

Kaleidoscope Night

If you are passing anywhere close to Bello Bar in Rathmines tomorrow evening pop in to catch us at Kaleidascope Night. We've been threatening to play at Dublin's favourite genre-bending bash for the last few years now, and finally it's happening!
We're in very good company too - you'll also get to hear Richard Strauss' Metamorphosen, Mozart's Duo No. 1 for violin and viola K.423, Byrd's Ave Verum Corpus, Éna Brennan's rupture ii, and a world premiere by Alma Kelliher.
