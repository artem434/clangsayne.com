10th January

Danish Tour 2019

We'll be on our Danish Tour from February 25th - March 2nd.

Monday 25th we play 5E - a hub for live improvised and experimental music, as part of Carolyn's month-long residency.

Thursday 28th we're at 'Lutter Ore' ; a new concert series at Peryton. Four established musicians from Copenhagen's experimental music scene present a wide and varied program focusing on free improvisation and contemporary compositional music.

Friday 1st March takes us to Tojhuset - Fredericia's premiere live music venue for jazz & contemporary music - for their 'Friday Night Jam'

Further details to follow.
