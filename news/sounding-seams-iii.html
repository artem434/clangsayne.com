22nd May

Sounding Seams III

I will be back in the Irish National Heritage Park in early June with Clang Sayne for the third event in my chimes project.
I've composed 4 new pieces based on sketches I made on the chimes for my performance in 2016, and I'll spend the week in the park with the rest of Clang Sayne rehearsing and recording them from Monday 6th – Fri 10th June. On Sat 11th we'll host a series of workshops for teens, followed by a short concert premiering the new works.
Really excited to be back playing again, and very much looking forward to our first gig with Caimin Gilmore on Sat 11th!

Supported by The Arts Council of Ireland; Music Generation Wexford & Wexford County Council arts Dept.
