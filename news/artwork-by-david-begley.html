Monday 11th September

Artwork by David Begley for The Round Soul

Very, very excited to know that as I type artist, David Begley is making a series of 7 prints in response to the 7songs on the 'Round Soul of the World'. Can't wait to see them at the end of the month! The image here is from his website - check out more of his beautiful work at www.davidbegley.com
The prints will be exhibited at Wexford Arts Centre on the 8th of October when we play there on our Autumn tour, and thereafter they'll be visable on the Clang Sayne website.
Watch this space!
