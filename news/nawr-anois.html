NAWR Anois Tuesday 24th July

Our UK tour kicks off at 'NAWR ANOIS!' at the BBC Hall in Swansea, Wales on Tuesday the 24th of July.
This is the last concert in a series of 7, showcasing experimental music from Ireland and Wales. We're sharing the bill with Aonghus McEvoy - Dublin based artists producing ululations via guitars, voices, tapes and detritus.
